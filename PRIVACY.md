# Privacy Policy

**mikMPD** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- No analytics or tracking
- No crash reporting services
- No third-party SDKs
- No data shared with anyone

All communication happens directly between the app and your MPD server on your local network. No data is sent to external servers, with the exception of optional album art and artist info lookups via MusicBrainz, Cover Art Archive, and Wikipedia — these are standard HTTP requests that do not include any personal or identifying information.

Settings and connection details are stored locally on your device.

## Third-Party Content

mikMPD may retrieve and display content from the following third-party services:

- **MusicBrainz** — Music metadata used to locate album art. Available under the [Creative Commons CC0 (public domain)](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/) license. See [MusicBrainz Social Contract](https://musicbrainz.org/doc/Social_Contract).
- **Cover Art Archive** — Album artwork sourced via the Cover Art Archive, a joint project of MusicBrainz and the Internet Archive. Images are provided under their respective licenses as specified by contributors.
- **Wikipedia** — Artist biographies and images retrieved from Wikipedia. Text content is available under the [Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-SA 4.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/) license. Images may be subject to individual licenses as specified on their Wikipedia file pages.

This content is fetched on demand, cached locally on your device, and is not redistributed.
